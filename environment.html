Environmental factors and statistics (from the report)

Environmental Factors and Statistics

Direct greenhouse gas emissions (2023)

In 2023, the Atyrau refinery's direct greenhouse gas emissions amounted to 1,583.2 thousand tons of CO₂ equivalent, which is lower than in the previous year. This indicates a reduction in emissions due to improved energy efficiency and environmental programs.

(ANPZ, 2024)

Emissions intensity

The intensity of emissions from oil refining is about 114 tons of CO₂ per 1,000 tons of raw materials, which is lower than the industry average. This demonstrates a gradual increase in the environmental efficiency of recycling.

(KazMunayGas, 2022)

Other refinery emissions

Emissions from oil refineries include CO₂, CO and nitrogen oxides, which have a negative impact on the environment and human health.

(Ivanov, Petrov & Smirnov, 2023)
