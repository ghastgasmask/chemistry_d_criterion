Global Impact

How Naphtha Reforming Addresses Critical Global Issues

1

Satisfication of Global Demand for Transportation Fuels

Naphtha reforming plays a role in satisfying the global demand for high-quality, efficient transportation fuels. By producing high-octane gasoline, it contributes to improved fuel economy and reduced overall fuel consumption.

High-Octane Gasoline Production

The process upgrades low-octane naphtha into high-octane gasoline components, making fuels suitable for modern engines.

Improved Economy

Higher octane fuels allow for greater engine compression ratios

Reduced Consumption

Naphtha reforming contributes to reduced overall fuel consumption globally.

2

Mitigating Environmental Pollution

The process has been mitigating environmental pollution. The ability to produce high-octane gasoline catalytically eliminated the need for lead-based anti-knock additives.

Elimination of Lead-Based Additives

Before

Lead-Based Anti-Knock Additives

  • Highly toxic to humans and environment
  • Environmental contamination
  • Public health hazards
  • Air pollution from vehicle emissions

After

Catalytic Production of High-Octane Gasoline

  • No need for toxic lead additives
  • Cleaner production process
  • Improved air quality
  • Enhanced public health worldwide
